HSK 1 - Lesson 1

Introduction

Welcome to Lesson 1 of HSK 1. In this lesson, we will focus on basic vocabulary and sentence structures. This is your first step to understanding and speaking Mandarin Chinese.

Learning Materials

Word Pinyin Meaning
你 nǐ You
好 hǎo Good
吗 ma Question particle
我 wǒ I, me
是 shì Am, is, are

Example Sentences:

  • 你好吗？(Nǐ hǎo ma?) - How are you?
  • 我是学生。(Wǒ shì xuéshēng.) - I am a student.
