HSK 1 - Lesson 4

Learn How to Introduce Family Members

In this lesson, we will learn how to introduce our family members in Mandarin. This is a practical and essential skill when talking about your personal life.

Family Member Vocabulary

  • 爸爸 (bà ba) - Father
  • 妈妈 (mā ma) - Mother
  • 哥哥 (gē ge) - Older brother
  • 姐姐 (jiě jie) - Older sister
  • 弟弟 (dì di) - Younger brother
  • 妹妹 (mèi mei) - Younger sister

Example Sentences

  • 这是我的妈妈。 (Zhè shì wǒ de mā ma.) - This is my mom.
  • 他是我的哥哥。 (Tā shì wǒ de gē ge.) - He is my older brother.
  • 我有一个弟弟。 (Wǒ yǒu yī gè dì di.) - I have a younger brother.
