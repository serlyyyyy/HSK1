HSK 1 - Lesson 3

Learn Numbers in Chinese

In this lesson, we will learn the numbers from 1 to 10 in Mandarin.

Chinese Numbers

  • 一 (yī) - One
  • 二 (èr) - Two
  • 三 (sān) - Three
  • 四 (sì) - Four
  • 五 (wǔ) - Five
  • 六 (liù) - Six
  • 七 (qī) - Seven
  • 八 (bā) - Eight
  • 九 (jiǔ) - Nine
  • 十 (shí) - Ten

Practice Activity

Write down the numbers in Mandarin and practice saying them out loud!
